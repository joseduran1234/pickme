    A CHIEF’S KISS
    Vertical Short Form
    Moon and Sun Production LLC
    Alta TV
    NON-UNION

    		 

    Producer: Yuchuan Cao
    Director: Zijian Su
    Writer: Johny Hazelnut
    Casting Director: Yuchuan Cao
    Audition: Via Eco Cast self tape
    Shoot Dates: March 28 - April 4 2025 (7 production days, April 2 is day off) 
    Rate of Pay: Varies, see roles
    Location: Los Angeles, CA

     
    DEADLINE: MARCH 28, 2025
     
    IF POSSIBLE, PLEASE SUBMIT ACTOR'S ONLINE DEMO CLIPS ALONG WITH EACH ACTOR SUBMISSION.
     
    [ HALEY RIVERS ]
    18 to 28; open ethnicity; woman. A talented and ambitious young chef with a passion for cooking and a strong sense of family loyalty. She graduated at the top of her class and dreams of carrying on her late mother’s culinary legacy at The Golden Table. Torn between her father’s revenge plot against Mark DiLaurentis and his competing restaurant and her own ambitions, she initially joins Mark as a spy but finds herself caught in a whirlwind of passion, betrayal, and self discovery. Haley is determined, kind-hearted, and sharp-witted, but her heart is conflicted between duty, love, and ambition. She dreams of forging her own path—standing in the heart of a blazing kitchen, flames and smoke in the air, as she crafts her next masterpiece together with her love. Role requires kissing scenes: an intimacy coordinator will be on set. Rate of Pay: $600/day. LEAD

    [ MARK DILAURENTIS ]
    30 to 45; open ethnicity; man. A world-renowned, billionaire chef with three Michelin stars and two American Culinary Cup wins. He has a reputation for being a ruthless perfectionist. Mark exudes a bad-boy charm with his tattoos, sharp tongue, and commanding presence in the kitchen. Behind his tough exterior lies a deeply passionate chef who values creativity and perfection in food. His heart has been hardened by past betrayal, particularly by his ex fiancée Dominique, making it difficult for him to trust others especially Haley, who reminds him of someone he once loved. Despite this, he finds himself drawn to her in ways he cannot explain. And for the first time, he surrenders control and lets his heart take the lead. Role requires kissing scenes: an intimacy coordinator will be on set.  Rate of Pay: $600/day. LEAD

    [ ADRIAN BLACKWELL ]
    25 to 35; open ethnicity; man. Haley’s wealthy and controlling boyfriend, a businessman who initially appears to be a supportive partner but gradually reveals possessive and manipulative tendencies. He wants Haley to quit her job and only cook for him, viewing her as part of his “perfect” future. When he senses her drifting toward Mark, he becomes increasingly jealous and domineering. His love for Haley is twisted by his need for control, leading him to drastic measures to keep her in his life. Rate of Pay: $300/day. SUPPORTING

    [ VANESSA ]
    22 to 32; open ethnicity; woman. A chef at Desire who initially befriends Haley but soon grows jealous of her rapid rise in the kitchen—and Mark’s growing attention toward her. She has an unrequited crush on Mark and sees Haley as competition. While she plays the role of a supportive friend, she’s a wildcard— capable of betrayal when it benefits her. She quietly rises from a dishwasher at Mark’s restaurant, to scheming together with Adrian to exact revenge and success as her own chef. Rate of Pay: $300/day. SUPPORTING

    [ JON RIVERS ]
    55 to 65; open ethnicity; man. Haley’s strict and ambitious father, the owner of The Golden Table, which was once the top restaurant in Los Angeles before Mark’s rise. Blinded by revenge, he pressures Haley into infiltrating Desire to steal Mark’s secret recipes and reclaim their former glory. While he genuinely loves his daughter, his obsession with defeating Mark clouds his judgment, making him manipulative and demanding. Rate of Pay: $200/day. DAY PLAYER

    [ DIEGO OSORIO ]
    25 to 38; open ethnicitiy; man. The charismatic and energetic manager of Desire. He admires Mark but isn’t afraid to call him out when needed. Diego is the bridge between Mark and the rest of the staff, maintaining order and handling business matters. Though friendly and humorous, he has a keen eye for betrayal and is always wary of people who might hurt Mark. Rate of Pay: $200/day. DAY PLAYER

     
    Storyline: A talented female chef enters the restaurant and is helped and recognized by the head chef in her life and her ability to work, and they go from being at odds to a loving relationship
