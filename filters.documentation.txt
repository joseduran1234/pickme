Introduction

This project is a an LLM agent for actors trying to break into Hollywood. It's meant to remove the friction and administrative work of applying to acting gigs in Los Angeles. 


Filters
- Breakdowns (submit projects posted on Actors Access)
	- Date, Title, Type, Casting Director, start date, union, region
- Photos & Slateshots (manage photos)
- About Me ( key profile info, special skills, union, role matching criteria)
- Auditions (auditions, callbacks, etc)
- Videos (manage video and audio)
- Cmail (connect with casting and talent representatives)
